Explore each room of this doll's house to find hidden facts about it.
×

Given the time period when the house is set, we didn't design a bathroom as we know it nowadays.

We painted a wooden bathtub in copper so it would give that effect and we added the furniture we thought you could find in a Georgian house bathroom.

×

Can you find the 3 cats hidden in the room? They are a nod to our own feline family.

You can also see on the sofa a miniature copy of Pride and Prejudice (a gift from our daughter) which was what inspired us to build the house.

×

We divided the living room in three areas: on the left there is a table with a tea party going on; on the middle a couple of sofas where people are just reading and relaxing; and a piano and arp on the right for some music.

You'll find a Mr Darcy over the chimney.

×

All the clothing on the house is handmade, like the curtains or the rug in this room as well as the tablecloth. We bought the furniture and decoration over the years in some fairs or online, and some nice details like empress Sisi in a plate on top of the chimney from Vienna.

You'll find Lizzie Bennet on a frame on top of the chimney.

×

We love to add historical details to our houses, like the bells on top of the door in the kitchen for the servants to be called or like the copper tins and pans to cook.

We've arranged the scene so the cook lady is busy preparing the food for the party upstairs.

×

As much as we wanted to keep the house consistent with the time period, we also wanted to be able to enjoy the house at any time, so Pablo added some lights.

We also paied close attention to details both when we watched movies or when we visited actual houses from the time period to design the house.

×

All the books but one are our usual "fake books" where we take a piece of wood and we cover it with wrapping paper to give them a real feel.

Silvia cross stitched the rug herself and we are aware there are two desks with different designs, but we are working on improving that part when inspiration hits us.
